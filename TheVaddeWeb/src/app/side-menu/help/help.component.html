How to Place an order

To place an order, navigate to the Menu Page and select the items you wish to order. You can increase or decrease the count of items using or signs. All the orders made by you can be viewed in the shopping_cart Icon after selection.
Search for your orders

In order to search for a specific item you can use our search box to enter the item you prefer. The Search bar can be found on the right side of the header layout beside the profile Icon.

How to make payments

The payment section allows you to checkout the item from the cart. Please make sure you are logged in before you make any payments. In the payment section you can add a new credit/debit card details for the first time and save your card for later if preferred.

How to edit your profile

You can edit your profile by logging into your account. Once you log in you can:

1. Change your personal information in the User Details section by using the Edit button.

2. You can view your order list and payment details.

3. You can add money into the V-wallet to make future payments - coming soon..

4. Add a Gift card if you have to get discounted price - coming soon..

How to Cancel Order

You can cancel your order within 20 minutes of your placed order and refund will be allocated once cancellation of order is confirmed.
